Vishnu Stationary

Vishnu Stationary, located in Mysore, started its journey in 1995 as a small family-owned business. Mr. Ramesh, the founder of the store, recognized the growing demand for stationery items in the local community and saw an opportunity to provide quality products and excellent customer service. With limited resources, Mr. Ramesh initially operated Vishnu Stationary from a small rented space in the bustling market area of Mysore. The store stocked a wide range of stationery supplies, including notebooks, pens, pencils, art materials, office supplies, and school essentials. Through word-of-mouth recommendations and dedicated customer service, Vishnu Stationary gradually gained popularity among the residents, schools, and businesses in Mysore. Mr. Ramesh's commitment to offering competitive prices, a diverse product selection, and personalized assistance ensured the store's steady growth and reputation.

The owner of Vishnu Stationary is Mr. Ramesh Kumar. Born and raised in Mysore, Mr. Ramesh comes from a family with a deep-rooted entrepreneurial spirit. Recognizing the potential in the stationery business, he decided to establish his own store and embarked on a journey to fulfill his dream. With a passion for providing quality stationery products and excellent customer service, Mr. Ramesh took on the role of the founder and owner of Vishnu Stationary in 1995. Over the years, Mr. Ramesh has played a pivotal role in shaping the success and growth of Vishnu Stationary. His hands-on approach and attention to detail have allowed the store to thrive in a competitive market. He personally selects the inventory, ensuring a diverse range of products that cater to the needs of different customers, whether they are students, artists, or professionals. Mr. Ramesh's commitment to building lasting relationships with customers and his dedication to delivering a seamless shopping experience have cemented Vishnu Stationary's position as a trusted name in the industry.
